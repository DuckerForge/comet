Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Comet ("the app", "we") is a non-custodial multichain swap and bridge application built for the Solana Seeker. We designed Comet to need as little of your data as possible. This policy explains what we do and do not collect.

1. Non-custodial by design

Comet never takes custody of your funds or your private keys. All transactions are signed on your device using the Seeker's Seed Vault. We cannot access, move, or freeze your assets.

2. Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or identity to use the app.
  • We do not store your private keys, seed phrase, or wallet credentials.
  • We do not require account creation or sign-up.

3. Information processed to perform a swap

To find routes and execute a swap, the app sends the minimum necessary data — such as the tokens, amounts, chains, and your public wallet address — to our liquidity provider, RocketX, and the relevant blockchains. Public blockchain transactions are, by nature, recorded on a public ledger.

4. Third-party services

Swaps are routed through the RocketX aggregator and executed on third-party exchanges and blockchains. Their handling of data is governed by their own terms and policies. See the RocketX website for details.

5. Analytics

If anonymous, aggregated usage analytics are used to improve the app, they will not include personal identifiers or wallet-level tracking. No data is sold.

6. Children

Comet is not directed at anyone under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "last updated" date.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at cismytb@gmail.com.
